Jaeseong Lee

I am a Ph.D candidate supervised by Jaegul Choo, at Kim Jaechul Graduate School of AI, KAIST. My research interest lies in reconstructing photo-realistic human heads with controllability. Particularly, my research topics include neural head avatar synthesis with rigging or relighting. I have worked on 2D face swapping with generative models, before.

I was a research intern at Meta Reality Labs with Carlos Aliaga. I collaborate with Marcel Buehler from ETH Zurich. I was a research intern at Naver AI Lab with Jin-Hwa Kim and Kakao Brain with Hyunjoon Lee. I completed my Ph.D coursework in Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence at KAIST, and a Bachelors in Engineering at DGIST.
